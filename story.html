THE STORY

Far off in the distant future, Earth finds istelf at war with a race of giant alien bugs. With no justification for being attacked, Earths only option is to bring the fight to the insects. The planet of Klendathu is a dry, desert planet, overpopulated with countless insect creatures, making the war for the Federation anything but easy. Follow the life of Johnny Rico and his friends as they go from unarmed civilians to citizens fighting together on a forein planet against a threat to the human race.

FUN FACTS

During the filming of Starship Troopers, it was reported that on the average day, about 25 cast or crew members would have to be treated for heatstroke.

Almost every uniform in the movie contains WW2 German military uniform refernces. The Federation flag also contains a bird which closely resembles the Imperial Eagle.

The scenes in the movie showing the destruction of Buenos Aires used actual footage of the Oakland Hills fire from October of 1991.

The Filming of Starship Troopers used more ammunition than any other movie had ever used before it. This is very noticable when watching the film.
