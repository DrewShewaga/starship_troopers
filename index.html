THE ORIGINAL CAST

Casper Van Dien

Johnny Rico

John D. "Johnny" Rico is an Argentinian infantryman in the Mobile Infantry. John Rico has become over his life in one of the most representative icons of all the United Citizen Federation.

Neil Patrick Harris

Carl Jenkins

General (Dr.) Carl Jenkins is a psychic, a general, a scientist and minister of the Ministry of Paranormal Warfare of Military Intelligence. Celebrity, sinister and popular man of the United Citizen Federation.

Denise Richards

Carmen Ibanez

Carmen Ibanez (Buenos Aires, Argentina) is an Argentinian Captain in the United Citizen Federation Fleet. She was a crewmember on the Corvette Transport Rodger Young.

Michael Ironside

Jean Rasczak

Rasczak is a seasoned veteran who has been decorated ten times for meritorious service. He is described as having "broken the mold" of trooper officer behavior.

Patrick Muldoon

Zander Barcalow

Zander Barcalow was a pilot onboard the Rodger Young until its destruction. He is a rival of Johnny Rico as they both want to be in a relationship with Carmen Ibanez.

Jake Busey

Ace Levy

Ace Levy is a private in the Mobile Infantry who joined the same training unit as Johnny Rico. During training he constantly competed against Johnny over which of the two will make Squad Leader.
